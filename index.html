SINGLETEXT

... is a small app that gives you a single text window for your note. Install it on your Mac, iPad, iPhone, or iPod touch; and your note is always kept up to date across all your devices. Easy.

SingleText works with iCloud. Just sign in to your iCloud account when you set up your Mac, iPad, iPhone, or iPod touch. And that's it. No other settings to configure, in fact, SingleText doesn't have settings.

Use it to share text between your devices. Your grocery list; to do items; the URL to that funny site; the phone number of that restaurant for dinner; the name of the girl that you just met. You don't need to worry about saving or filenames; just tap and type.

Mac

Use SingleText for Mac to share URLs, text snippets, or phone numbers from your Mac to your iOS devices. Do you have a phone number that you want to call? Just copy it into the text window, quit the app, and open SingleText on your iPhone and tap the number. Easy.

SUPPORT
FAQ

What are the system requirements?

SingleText requires Mac OS X Lion (10.7.2) and iOS 5 with iCloud account (Documents & Data sync enabled).

How long do I need to wait until the text is synchronized?

Generally, it's not very long. The text is synchronized almost immediately after you have finished the editing. But depending on the network traffic and iCloud, it may take up to 30 seconds for your other devices to receive the new text.

How do I save or synchronize the text when I'm using SingleText for Mac?

SingleText automatically saves text changes every ten seconds or when you quit the application.

How much text can I write with SingleText?

About one page of text or roughly about 500 words. SingleText is designed to store a small amount of text that you need to share between devices. Do not use SingleText to write a book, unless it's a very tiny book.

How does SingleText resolve text conflicts?

It doesn't. It assumes that you want to keep the newest text. Normally, this is what people want.

Then, why do I want to use SingleText?

Three words: simplicity, speed, and synchronization. You just need to tap and type; without worrying about saving, filenames, or other settings. And your text will be available on your Mac, iPad, iPhone, and iPod touch. Effortless!
